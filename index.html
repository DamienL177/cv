ABOUT ME

I wondered for quite some time what kind of work I would do, what kind I should do. Interested in video games, I chose in high school to try my hands at computing. It was love at first sight.

After high school, I joined the IUT of Bayonne and the Basque Country for a formation in Computer Science. I discovered a lot of work domains I wasn't aware of and learned a lot of skills, both soft and hard ones. When the time came for my internship, I joined Alcyons, a small software creation studio my two brothers interned with. At the same time, I chose to leave the IUT to join an engineer school.

The ENSEEIHT has a course in Digital Sciences with apprenticeship and I took the opportunity. When I told my boss in Alcyons, he offered me to continue my work by doing the apprenticeship with Alcyons and here I am.
