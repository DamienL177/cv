INTERNATIONAL MOBILITY

While I do appreciate staying home in France, I'd like to, one day, go and take a look at what other countries have to offer.

SPAIN

I visited Spain two times during my teenage years.


The first time I went with a friend of mine and their family to Extremadura in the south-west of the country. I had a good time discovering the Roman history of the country in person.


The second time was a kind of student exchange program that happened when I was 14. We went a week to Zaragoza and while we didn't study much in spanish we visited a lot of places.
